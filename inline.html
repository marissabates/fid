<h2>Low and Slow</h2>
<p>This week i am <em>extremely</em> excited about a new 
cooking technique called <dfn><i>sous vide</i></dfn>. In <i>sous vide</i>
cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed 
in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely 
set to the target temperature you want the food 
to be cooked to. In his book, <cite>Cooking for Geeks</cite>, 
Jeff Potter describes it as <q>ultra-low-temperature poaching.</q></p>
